Update: Fundraiser is closed! Thank you all!

Hello all!

First of all, I want to thank everyone who joined the walkie-talkie fundraiser! I managed to make the payment before the exchange rate increased 😉 The guys will receive radios early next week: 32 walkie-talkies with additional batteries 📻 Not 35 pcs. as planned, but the manufacturer itself rejected 3 pcs. So the boys will have 53 walkie-talkies in total, which is not bad! Thank you all again!

🤕 Yesterday I received news - some guys are in the hospital after the battle that they got in during scouting. To be honest, such messages are always difficult and sad to receive. But, thanks God, they are alive, the injuries are not serious, the boys are rushing into battle again! 👊 I was pleasantly surprised that no less than 10 orcs went to the Kobzon concert 🎶, I hope they all go there!

📹 In addition to everything, there was one trouble - the thermal imager was lost during the battle. This, unfortunately, happens, but the main thing is that the guys are alive. Soldiers need the Pulsar Helion 2 XP50 Pro thermal imager - a cool thing that helps you see clearly those non-humans at a long distance, even at night.

💰 I am asking you for help in raising funds for a thermal imager. Its price is 3400 euros. I believe that together we will be able to support our soldiers so that when they return to the front, they will send even more racist monsters to hell! 🔥

🙏 Please join and ↗️ Share this page so other people can join!

Thank you very much in advance!!!

Glory to the Armed Forces! 🪖 Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
