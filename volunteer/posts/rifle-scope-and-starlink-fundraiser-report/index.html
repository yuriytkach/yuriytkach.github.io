Rifle Scope and Starlink Fundraiser Report 📡 📝

Jan 10, 2023

← Back to all posts

Thank you all for taking part in fundraiser for sniper rifle scope and Starlink device for Ukrainian soldiers.

It took some time to find the scope with a good price. I first wanted to get it in the same store where I bought previous scope, but for two reasons I didn’t do it - the store appeared to be not reliable and the price was even higher than before.

Thanks to other volunteers from Poland and Netherlands I was able to find the scope for the great price, much cheaper than I’ve initially expected!

As for the Starlink - I was able to order it directly and the price is not that high as if you order it in Europe with transfer to Ukraine afterwards. The device is already shipped to me and should arrive soon. As soon as soldiers receive it I will post more photos here.

📊 A few numbers.

Total amount collected in different currencies: €2,827.

The money was spent on the following:

  • Rifle scope: €2,391

  • Starlink: $435

All the payments were done in Ukrainian currency, and due to conversion I've actually spend the whole amount that was raised.

Thanks very much again to everyone!

Glory to the Armed Forces! 🪖 Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
