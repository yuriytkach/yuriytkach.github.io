Night Vision &amp; Rifle Scope Fundraiser Report ⚡📝

Published Nov 01, 2022

Back to all posts

Finally, the devices were handled over to soldiers and I can happily make this report for the fundraiser for night vision device and sniper rifle scope.

Thank you everyone who participated in this fundraiser. 🤗 You’re all great and it would never be possible without your help!

It took a bit longer for me to get this report done, because of big waiting time for the devices to be shipped. Me and my friends had some unpleasant communication with the store that sells them and we had to get help from many other volunteers and even some officials to actually get things moving.

Eventually, everything was done good, and I was even able to get good discount for devices! And the excess was spent on buying some additional equipment.

I personally handled everything to our soldies: sniper rifle scope with era-tac mount to soldiers of 47th brigade, and night vision devices with other equipment to soldiers of 72nd brigade. 💪

📊 A few numbers.

Total amount collected in different currencies: €6,545.

The money was spent on the following:

  • Rifle scope: €2,711.16

  • Nigh vision PVS-14: €2,727.68

  • Scope mount Recknagel ERA-TAC 20 MOA: 1,610 zł

  • Scope mount Recknagel ERA-TAC 0 MOA: €306

  • Scope mount ATN Quick Detach: €140

  • Spare batteries for thermal monocular: €193.95

All the payments were done in UAH at different dates, so exchange rates were different, plus there were some bank commissions for transfers. Total amount spent ~€6,420. Therefore we have an excess of €125, which goes right away to our next fundraiser!

Thanks very much again to everyone!

Glory to the Armed Forces! 🪖 Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
