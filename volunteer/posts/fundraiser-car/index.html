Fundraiser for pickup truck 🚙 for Ukrainian soldiers

Nov 04, 2022

← Back to all posts

Update: Fundraiser is closed! Thank you all!

Dear friends! I have an urgent emergency fundraiser for the heroes of the Ukrainian Armed Forces!

Since the times of Cossack Ukraine, a Cossack's best friend was his horse. It was always there: both in battle and in everyday life, which is why the soldiers called their horses - “brothers”. Today this role belongs to the car or pickup truck!

It so happened that at the moment the guys urgently need at least one "horse". Our heroes are made of steel, but they get wounded... The enemy has destroyed many vehicles, and the unit has none left that can at least take out the wounded!

Time is priceless and the need is critical - please, help to raise money for a pickup truck 🙏

I am asking for help for an urgent purchase, and even a small contribution will help SAVE THE MOST PRICELESS - LIFE!

Thank you very much in advance!!!

Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
