Report for additional help for Ukrainian Soldiers 📝

Jan 19, 2023

← Back to all posts

This is a small report on additional purchases to help Ukrainian soldiers 📝

1️⃣ For the unit of 72nd brigade the following was purchased:

  • 🧥 winter uniform (warms the boys for 3 weeks already);

  • 🛞 winter tires for the pick-up truck that we’ve bought to them recently thanks to your help.

I’ve conducted a small fundraiser for that on my Facebook page. A total of UAH 24,429 was collected. I added my own funds in the amount of UAH 15,000, totaling UAH 39,430, - that was enough to get uniform and tires! Thank you all so much for participating!

2️⃣ For brigade 47, I did not announce the fundraiser, but this month I bought: 20 pcs of magazines for rifles and 1 buttstock for a sniper rifle. This purchase - in the amount of 41,000 UAH - was fully paid for by the funds of my family, to whom I am infinitely grateful for their tireless moral and material support!

Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
