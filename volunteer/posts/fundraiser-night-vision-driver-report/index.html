Report for fundraiser for nightvision for driver 📝

Published Jun 25, 2023

Back to all posts

Thank you everyone who joined the fundraiser for the night vision device for a driver of the evacuation pick up! Need I say how important this purchase was? Saving the priceless lives of our heroes is a huge contribution to our Victory and a manifestation of our respect for their feat!

The device was sent to the soldiers already this week, and the guys are extremely pleased!

I sincerely thank you all from myself and from our soldiers! Together we go to the Victory, and the day is approaching when there will be no invaders on our land!

📊 Some numbers:

A total of €1,185 was collected in different currencies. I’ve covered the remaining amount and was able to purchase the device for €1220.

From myself and from the military, I sincerely thank everyone for your help!

Together to victory! 💪 Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦 Слава Україні! 🇺🇦
