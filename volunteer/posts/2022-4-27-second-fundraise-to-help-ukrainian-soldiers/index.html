Second Fundraiser 💰 to Help Ukrainian Soldiers

Published Apr 26, 2022

Back to all posts

Update: Fundraiser is closed! Thank you all!

Dear Friends!

I received feedback from the military guys on the front line about the equipment we've bought together a month ago with your financial help. Guys thank you very much! Equipment is great and helps them a lot! 💪

💛💙Now the boys asked again for very important help. Unfortunately, the terrible war is not over and fighting is harder every day, equipment is needed more and more expensive. So I ask you to help raise funds, as well as to spread information among your friends with a call for help.

This time we would need to get the following:

  • 📻 walkie-talkies

  • 🔋 batteries for drones

  • 🛰️ drone with thermal imaging camera

The amount is huge, around 15000 euros, but I believe that it is real to raise it with your help!
All purchases I do personally at various EU stores and then deliver to Ukraine with help of my friends.

The equipment is intended for a specific unit of the Ukrainian Armed Forces, which is fighting in the Luhansk region - exactly where the heaviest fighting is taking place now.

🙏 Please, join. Any donation, regardless of size, brings us closer to achieving our goal.

↗️ Please, share this page so other people can join!

THANK YOU VERY MUCH in advance!!!

Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦

Why Exactly This Equipment?

It’s true that other governments that support Ukraine send us weapons and humanitarian help. We’re very thankful to them! However, some specific equipment that give extra advantage to our soldiers is not provided. Additionally, that extra equipment helps to safe lives. Therefore, I’ve joined the huge volunteering effort to help Ukrainian soldiers win and stop this bloody war.
