Preliminary report 📝 and few words about drones 🛰️

Published May 06, 2022

Back to all posts

Friends, you are awesome! Thanks to all of you, we’ve managed to raise almost 4K euro in a week! I’ve added my own funds and bought 10 walkie-talkies, which are already going to Poland, and also I’ve made a pre-payment and booked 9 drone batteries at a very attractive price!

Thank you to everyone who joins! The soldiers are confident in us, so I urge you not to stop, because I really want to fulfill, perhaps, their main wish - to buy a drone with a thermal imaging camera. It costs about 7-9K euros (depending on the success of the search, because drones are currently in short supply). This is a large amount, but I believe that we will be able to collect it. Do know that each donation, regardless of size, brings us closer to achieving our goal!

🛰️ Why is a drone so important? Drones are actively used by our soldiers for various purposes. Of course, more often it is intelligence, because when you know where, who and with what is sitting, it is easier to "visit" them. But our clever guys have adapted drones for additional tasks. With the help of drones, they aim to deliver various explosive "gifts" just to the trenches and heads of our enemies. So called "delivery of the future"! 😀 Videos of such “courier” deliveries have already started to appear on the Internet 📦

To make this tactic even more effective, as well as for other important tasks, our guys need a drone that can "see" the thermal signatures of enemies at night. That is a drone with a thermal imaging camera. At the moment, it is not very easy to find them on sale, because there are very few of them, and many have already been bought for Ukraine (which, in fact, is very good!). I found one in Spain and managed to keep it in reserve for us for some time. So all is left is to raise funds.

A few days ago, I had the honor of receiving some photos of our defenders from the front-line, which show the equipment that we together bought last time during last fundraiser. After seeing photos and speaking with the guys, I can say only this: they are fighting in such conditions that it is difficult to watch, the heart is breaking! But they smile… You see, they smile even living in dugouts, under round-the-clock shelling… Because they are Ukrainians, because they are free, because they are the BEST! They feel our support from behind, and because of that they have an extraordinary, superhuman strength… And this strength helps them to defend us again and again until the victory! 💙 💛

I and the guys are infinitely grateful to everyone who joins our fundraiser and helps to beat the occupiers! Together we can bring the victory closer! Each donation is both a declaration of faith in our defenders and a step towards the equipment needed in the war. So I invite everyone to join!

Also, if you can, please share my post (this, or the original post about fundraiser) so that more people can see it and take part in a good cause! Together we are a force! Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦

Kyiv, Ukraine
