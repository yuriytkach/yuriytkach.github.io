Thermal Monocular Fundraiser Report 📹📝

Published Aug 24, 2022

Back to all posts

Today, I congratulate every Ukrainian with the Independence Day! 🇺🇦

This day has always been special for me for several reasons, and this year, as for most of us, it took on an additional meaning 💙💛

Today I had the great honor to personally receive greetings from our defenders 🪖, drink coffee with them and thank them for their dedication to the fight for our freedom. The thermal monocular and some other things were handed over to the guys, and they headed to the East today to do some hot stuff 🔥

Friends, from myself and our heroes, I thank you all for your participation in the fundraiser. With the help of my friends in Europe I was able to get it with some discount, so the rest will go to the next fundraiser. Thank you all. You are incredible! 💪

📊 A few numbers.

Total amount collected in different currencies: €3250.

This money was spent on the following:

  • 📹 thermal monocular: €3050

We are plus €200. This excess will go to our next fundraiser, because unfortunately the war is not over yet and Ukrainian soldiers need our support.

Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
