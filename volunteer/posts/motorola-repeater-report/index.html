Motorola Repeater Fundraiser Report

Published Mar 01, 2023

Back to all posts

Hello everyone! Finally, a report for the repeater fundraiser.

I am happy to announce 2 great news. The first: we collected the full amount for the purchase of a Motorola repeater for the 47th brigade of the Armed Forces of Ukraine! This device was extremely important to the guys, and besides, it was not easy found on sale in an attractive combination of price / technical characteristics. I was lucky enough to find it and was able to pay quickly thanks to your help.

So the guys already have the device - it serves, helps and protects. They convey their sincere gratitude to everyone involved in the fundraiser!

The second: we collected a larger amount than was planned 😀 After counting all the donations, it turned out that we had an excess of 477 euros. They came in very handy, and from these funds I paid for the purchase of two Motorola AES licenses for the 48th battalion of the 72nd brigade (I bought the walkie-talkies for them in January).

So, we did a good deed again, bringing our common Victory closer! 💪 I am sincerely grateful to everyone who finds the opportunity and strength, despite the exhausting war and longing for innocent victims, to support each other and our Heroes. We have each other, and this is our irresistible power!

Let's support our glorious soldiers, let's love Ukraine, and let's be a free people forever.

Together to victory! Glory to Ukraine! Glory to the Armed Forces!!! 🇺🇦

📊 Numbers:

  • Total amount raised: 3477 €

  • Motorola repeater: 3000 € Delivery: 35 €

  • Motorola AES license HKVN4241 (2 pieces): 460,00 €
