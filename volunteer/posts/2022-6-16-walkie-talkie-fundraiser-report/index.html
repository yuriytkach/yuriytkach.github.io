Walkie-Talkie Fundraiser Report 📻📝

Jun 15, 2022

← Back to all posts

Finally, the walkies-talkies were delivered to our soldiers and I can make this report. Thank you very much to everyone who joined the fundraiser! 🤗

📻 I have bought 14 radios Retevis RT81 and one programming cable for them on Spanish Amazon. Interestingly, that on the day that I've made a purchase, the price dropped a bit, so I've managed to get a small discount! Yay!

📦 The radios were delivered to my friend in Netherlands. And with his help they were pretty quickly transferred to Ukraine. Again, the speed is astonishing - in less than 2 weeks from purchase the radios were delivered to soldiers on the front line.

If you remember those radios were initially intended for the guys that were strengthening our northern borders. However, last week their unit was transferred to the Eastern part of Ukraine, because help is needed there. So, now those radios will serve on the front line. 💪

📊 A few numbers.

Total amount that was raised: €1563
These money were spent on the following:

  • Radios (14 pcs), programming cable, and delivery: €1212

  • Fuel and repair for soldiers' car: UAH 10,000 (~ €290)

This time we are plus €61 🙌 This excess will go to our next fundraiser, as unfortunately the war is not over and Ukrainian soldiers need our support.

Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
