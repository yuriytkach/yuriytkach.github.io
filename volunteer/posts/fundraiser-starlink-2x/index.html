Fundraiser 2 x Starlink Kits 📡

Jul 19, 2023

← Back to all posts

Update: Fundraiser is closed! Thank you all!

Greetings!

A few months ago, we bought a Starlink for the troops of the 72nd brigade. The friendly equipment faithfully served on the front line, helping to destroy the orcs.

Recently, the boys came under massive fire 💣 The bad news: the Starlink was riddled with debris in several places, and it died with honor. The good news: all the boys survived! And they also gave the remains of the Starlink to a fellow craftsman, who extracted all the suitable microchips from it... And now they fly through the Donetsk steppes in small “birds” that bring the orcs to an inglorious end. ⚡

So the rested Starlink continued the service in a modified form, and transmitted to us that two should come in its place!

📡 I am inviting you to join a fundraiser for 2 devices at once. It's very difficult for the guys on the front lines without communication, and things like this are very vulnerable. So let's help restore and strengthen the capabilities of our heroes! It's not just the deaths of orcs, but the lives of our world's best defenders saved!

Amount to be raised - UAH 36,000. I believe that we will be able to close it quickly. Infinite gratitude to everyone for their help! You are sincere, strong, free! 💪 💛💙
