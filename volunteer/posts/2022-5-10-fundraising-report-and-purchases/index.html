Fundraising Report and Purchases 🛒

May 08, 2022

← Back to all posts

As of May 8, we collected UAH 177,288 in various currencies, namely UAH 84899, $680 and €2326.

All the funds raised so far have gone to the following expenses:

  • 10 Hytera HP705 VHF walkie-talkies for €435 plus delivery for €150. Total €4500. (📦 from France to Poland)

  • 9 batteries for drones (5 for DJI FPV and 4 for Mavic 2) for UAH 37,687 (📦 from Ukraine)

  • 5 pairs of Lowa Innox Pro GTX summer tactical shoes with delivery for €705. (📦 from Germany to Poland)

  • 3 pairs of active headphones Walker's Razor Slim Electronic Muff for $128 plus delivery for $17 (📦 from the USA to Ukraine)

  • 7 tactical gloves for UAH 3,150 (📦 from Ukraine)

A total of UAH 210,861 was spent. This is all the funds received from you + my own. Thanks for the help!

🔋 The most important thing is that we managed to buy drone batteries just in Ukraine, and they have already been handed over to the guys on the front-line together with gloves.

👟 The shoes have already arrived in Poland and are awaiting delivery to Ukraine.

📻 The walkie-talkies are going to Poland right now.

🎧 Headphones are already flying to Ukraine with the help of Nova Poshta.

But we do not stop and continue to raise funds to buy a drone with a thermal imaging camera 🛰️. Model currently being considered for purchase: DJI Mavic 2 Enterprise Advanced. We need to raise about 7000 euros. This is a very large sum, but I believe that we will be able to collect it. Know that each donation, regardless of size, brings us closer to achieving our goal!

As you may have heard in the news, Biden will sign a “Lend-Lease” tomorrow, May 9th. And let's make a "Ukrainian-Present" and raise funds to buy a drone for our guys! 😉

THANK YOU VERY MUCH in advance!!!

Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
