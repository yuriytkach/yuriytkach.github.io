Dear, friends! I announce a mega-fundraiser for Armed Forces of Ukraine fighters of TWO units at once!

Since the beginning of the active phase of war, I’ve raised funds and helped two units - according to the arrival of requests from them. So far, the boys have contacted me almost at the same time... I have no priorities, and the request of every soldier of the Armed Forces is sacred. So this fundraiser will be big, but extremely important! 🤨

⚡ I am opening a fund raiser for a night vision device and a sniper rifle scope. MINIMUM needed by fighters - 1 scope and 3 devices. It is very important to complete it - at least gradually in parts. At the first stage, let's buy 1 piece each. As soon as we collect them, we will continue to raise funds for 2 more devices. So, in the first part of a big purchase for two units we need to raise 6500 euros.

🙏 Please support us with a donation - every penny will protect our soldiers, give them more strength and advantages. ↗️ Also join in by reposting to spread information about the fundraiser as much as possible.

Any of your help will be a thick nail in the enemy's coffin and an invaluable contribution to our Victory!
