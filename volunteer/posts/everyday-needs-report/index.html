Report on fundraiser for different needs of ZSU units 📝

May 15, 2023

← Back to all posts

Thank you to everyone who participated in the collection of funds for various things for the units of the Armed Forces of Ukraine!

Thanks to all of you, I was able to raise the necessary amount and buy the items that the military asked for, and which were difficult to find at a reasonable price in an adequate time. By now, everything has already been purchased and handed over to our defenders.

📊 Some numbers!

A total of UAH 71,332 was collected in different currencies.

The funds were spent on the following:

  • 2 Motorola DP4401E walkie-talkies - UAH 41,800.

  • Subscription fee for 4 months of using Starlink - $300

  • Thermal imager Hikmicro LH19 - UAH 18,300.

The walkie-talkies went to the 48th battalion of the 72nd brigade. The previously transferred Starlink is also used there. In addition, I also handed over headphones and power banks.

The thermal imager was purchased with additional funding and given to a soldier of the 127th battalion of the 112th brigade.

From myself and from the military, I sincerely thank everyone for your help!

Together to victory! 💪 Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦 Слава Україні! 🇺🇦
