Update 2: Fundraiser is closed! Thank you all!

Updated 2022-06-23 with fund goal

This is the fundraiser to buy equipment for Ukrainian soldiers that fight on the front line with russian fascists! Unfortunately, this terrible war is not over and army needs our help!

The previous fundraisers were successful and I thank a lot to everyone who joined! You can read the reports about them by following the links at the bottom of the page.

The guys where we delivered radios last time asked for some additional equipment for them: spare batteries, longer antennas, extra chargers, etc. I have found all the things in stores and now we need to raise funds.

🙏 Please, join. Any donation, regardless of its size, brings us closer to our goal!

↗️ Please, share this page so other people can join!

THANK YOU VERY MUCH in advance!!!

Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
