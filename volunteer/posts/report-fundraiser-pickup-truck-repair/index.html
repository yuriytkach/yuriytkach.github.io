Reports for Aug-Sep fundraisers 📝

Sep 26, 2023

← Back to all posts

I am elated to announce the successful conclusion of our fundraiser aimed at facilitating the repair of several vehicles for one unit of the 72nd brigade of the Armed Forces of Ukraine! The soldiers are now better equipped, and I am here to present this report with immense gratitude.

Firstly, a heartfelt thank you to every individual who contributed! Your generosity has made a significant impact, and the donations received have been instrumental in aiding our soldiers. The vehicles are now in optimal condition, ensuring the smooth execution of tasks and safeguarding the lives of our brave soldiers.

📊 Some numbers:

We managed to raise a total of UAH 68,187! The allocation of the funds is as follows:

  • Car Repairs: UAH 53,800

  • Car Fuel: UAH 13,500

The meticulous allocation ensured that every hryvnia was spent judiciously, addressing the immediate needs of the unit. The excess amount of UAH 887 has been directed towards our next fundraiser, ensuring a head start for our forthcoming initiatives.

Additional Fundraiser Report

I also had the honor of running another noteworthy fundraiser on the HelpUA portal. This initiative aimed at procuring Starlink for the 3rd battalion of the operational assignment "FREEDOM" of the offensive brigade of NGU "Rubizh". This technology is pivotal in enhancing communication capabilities, ensuring seamless coordination and operational efficiency.

Closing Remarks

The unwavering spirit and the unity displayed by all contributors have been the cornerstone of these successful fundraisers. It is your support that empowers our soldiers, fortifying them as they defend our land and our values.

Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦 Glory to the Heroes! 🪖
