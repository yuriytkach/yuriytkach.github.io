Equipment Fundraiser Report 🔋📝

Jul 04, 2022

← Back to all posts

Fundraising for additional equipment for previously purchased walkie-talkies was successful, so I can report back! Thank you so much to everyone who participated! 🤗

I managed to order additional batteries, antennas, chargers from the manufacturer, as well as take a few more walkie-talkies! All purchased items are already on their way to my friend in the Netherlands! Also purchased some additional equipment requested by the military.

Below you can see invoices for the purchases. As soon as all packages arrive I will update this post with pictures.

📊 A few numbers.

Total amount collected: €1191.

This money was spent on the following:

  • 🔋 batteries (14 pcs.): $210

  • 📻 walkie-talkies (7 pcs.): $455

  • ⚡ block chargers for walkie-talkies (2 pcs.): $55

  • 📡 antennas (16 pcs.): $40

  • 🧤 gloves (4 pcs.): $76

  • 🌞 solar charging: $38

  • 🎧 active headphones: $40

  • 📦 shipping: $125

Total spent amount is $1039, or €995. And we are in plus again by €196 🙌 This excess will go to our next fundraiser, because unfortunately the war is not over yet and Ukrainian soldiers need our support.

Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
