Additional Walkie-Talkies Fundraiser Report 📻📝

Jul 23, 2022

← Back to all posts

Hello everyone and thank you! 🤗

I want to thank everyone who participated in the fundraiser for additional walkie-talkies! I managed to order 32 walkie-talkies with additional batteries from the manufacturer. The walkie-talkies have already arrived to my friend in the Netherlands and will be in Ukraine very soon. I also bought some more active headphones, because they are a necessary thing: as it turned out, they helped the boys a lot during the battle!

📊 A few numbers.

Total amount collected in different currencies: €2731.

This money was spent on the following:

  • 📻 radios with batteries (32 pcs.): $2368

  • 📦 delivery: $302

  • 🎧 active headphones (5 pcs.): $200

A total of $2,870, or €2,750 was spent (these exchange rates jump like crazy).

Thanks again to everyone!

Glory to the Armed Forces! 🪖 Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
