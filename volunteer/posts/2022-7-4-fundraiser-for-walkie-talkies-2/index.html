Fundraiser for Additional Walkie-Talkies for Ukrainian Armed Forces 📻

Jul 04, 2022

← Back to all posts

Update: Fundraiser is closed! Thank you all!

Dear friends! The guys who are currently defending Ukraine and its freedom in the east near Bakhmut have given very favorable reviews about the Retevis walkie-talkies that we bought last time. They completely satisfy soldiers, because they are digital, with encrypted communication, quite powerful and water-proof. The boys go scouting with them, and the walkie-talkies perform their function perfectly 💪

We’ve managed to raise funds and buy additional batteries, antennas, etc. for radios. But now I need your help 🙏

The guys asked to find 150 more such radios 😳 to equip their entire unit. This is a large number, and as I wrote before, due to problems with semiconductors, radios are not made in big quantities.

🤝 At the moment, I managed to negotiate directly with the manufacturer Retevis, and they booked the last lot they have in stock in the amount of 35 walkie-talkies with additional batteries. If we don't delay the payment, these walkie-talkies can be delivered in 2 weeks (The manufacturer will produce new radios, but it will take from one to two months 🤷‍♂️)

💰 Currently we need to collect 3000 euros - this is 35 radios, 35 batteries, and delivery to Europe.

🙏 Please join! Every donation, regardless of size, brings us closer to achieving the goal!

↗️ Please, share so that more people can join!

Thank you very much in advance!!!

Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
