Fundraiser for Walkie-Talkies 📻

Published May 31, 2022

Back to all posts

View Donations

Update: Fundraiser is closed! Thank you all!

After a successful fundraiser for a drone I appeal to you again to help raise funds to provide one Ukrainian military unit with additional equipment. These are the guys who are strengthening our northern borders right now, so that no more enemies can get through.

📻 They need walkie-talkies, not necessarily cool, but preferably digital.

As for walkie-talkies (I'm almost an expert 😁 now), I have managed to find a Retevis RT81 model for 90 euros apiece. These are not as cool as Motorola, which is nowhere to be found, but also digital, i.e. you can encrypt the channel, powerful enough, have the necessary protection against moisture and shock. The guys approved them, so for a bit more than € 1,300 we can buy as many as 15 pieces!

💰 Therefore, a total of € 1,400 needs to be raised to pay for radios and delivery. I believe that we can do that, because with the drone we have already proved that together we are a force 💪 And that every donation, regardless of size, brings us closer to achieving the goal!

🙏 Please, join.

↗️ Please, share this page so other people can join!

THANK YOU VERY MUCH in advance!!!

Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦
