Pickup Truck Fundraiser Report 🛻📝

Published Dec 01, 2022

Back to all posts

The fundraiser for pickup truck is over, soldiers have received the car and I can make this report!

First of all, thank you all for your help! You’re all great and all your donations were super invaluable! This car was very needed by Ukrainian soldiers and it is already on the front-line helping them do their tasks and save their lives!

We initially planned to find, buy and deliver the car from abroad. However, a bit later we found a car here in Ukraine, that only needed a small repair. It appeared to cost less, thus I’ve adjusted the fundraiser goal and changed its currency to UAH for better tracking. 😉

So, in total we were able to raise UAH 254,579 from the needed UAH 250K! The excess UAH 4,579 went to the purchase of winter uniform for the guys!

Below you can see picture of the soldiers of 72nd brigade with their new car in the East of Ukraine defending our country from russian occupiers.

Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦 Glory to Ukrainian Armed Forces! 🪖
