Report on First Fundraiser

Published Apr 09, 2022

Back to all posts

Thank you everyone for your donations! I was able to get thermal monocular, secure radios and accumulators for drones! It was not an easy scavenger hunt, but with the help of friends from different countries we've managed that! Early next week they will be delivered to guys in Ukraine!

But the fund raise continues, as I want to get one or more thermal monoculars while they are still available for the good price! These are super needed devices, not cheap and can break in those harsh conditions where they are used. Thus, the more we can get to the guys on the front line, the better!

Thus, we you still want and can join the fund raise, please do! Thank you very much!
