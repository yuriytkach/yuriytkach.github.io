Thermal Rifle Scope Fundraiser Report 🎯📝

Published Sep 08, 2022

Back to all posts

Thank you everyone! The fundraiser for thermal rifle scope was successful!

We’ve managed to gather the required amount quickly and I’ve ordered the scope. I must say that I was able to negotiate a very good discount for it, so I’ve adjusted the fundraiser goal.

Currently, the scope is on it’s way to Poland and from there it will head to Ukraine in short time. I will post here photos as soon as guys get the device.

📊 Total amount collected in different currencies: €1758.

Amount spent on the scope is €1620. So we have excess of €138 that goes right away to our new big epic fundraiser! ⚡

Thank you all again and Glory to Ukraine and its Heroes! 🇺🇦
