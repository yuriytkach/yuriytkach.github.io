Report for FPV drones 📝

Published Nov 30, 2023

Back to all posts

Our kamikaze drone FPV fundraiser was a success! 👍 Huge thanks to everyone who participated! Your support is what drives us forward to Victory. The drones have already been paid for, and we are now waiting for the delivery of the components 📦. Soon they will be collected and sent to where they are most wanted.

The rest of the funds were transferred to the current fundraiser for the car for drone operators who will be using these drones 😉 Join us, because mobility is very important for the efficiency of their work!

📊 A total of €3327 was collected in various currencies. From them, spare parts and work on the collection of drones were paid for in the amount of UAH 119,000. The balance of UAH 10,000 was transferred to the car tax.

Once again, many thanks to all of you for your help!
