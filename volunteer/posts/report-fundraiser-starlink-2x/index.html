Report for 2 x Starlink Kits 📝

Published Aug 07, 2023

Back to all posts

From me and from Ukrainian soldiers of the 72nd brigade I want to thank everyone for your help in raising funds for two Starlink kits. Reliable and fast communication is very crucial for our defenders.

One kit was needed urgently, thus I’ve bought it in Ukraine. For the second kit soldiers told me that they can wait and therefore I’ve ordered it in Europe. And since there were some discounts there it came out cheaper, which is great!

📊 Some numbers:

A total of 32,045 UAH was collected in different currencies. They were spent on the following:

  • 17,800 UAH - First Starlink kit, that was bought in Ukraine

  • €222 - Second Starlink kit, that was bought in France

  • 2,020 UAH - Delivery and customs tax for second kit

  • $20.09 - Additional payment for transfer of second Starlink kit to Ukrainian address

  • $75 - Additional payment for transfer of first Starlink kit to Ukrainian address

The total amount spent is 32,695 UAH. I’ve covered the shortage myself.

Once again I thank everyone for your help!

Together to victory! 💪 Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦 Слава Україні! 🇺🇦
