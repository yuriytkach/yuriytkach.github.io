Another Thermal Scope Fundraiser Report 🎯🎯📝

Published Oct 30, 2022

Back to all posts

Thank you everyone for your help in raising funds to buy another thermal rifle scope for a soldier of the 48th assault battalion of the 72 brigade of Ukrainian Armed Forces!

Together with the scope I was able to buy the special mount for it.

After consultations with the soldier it was decided to get a different model of the scope, which appeared to be slightly cheaper and had same characteristics as needed.

The purchase was financed partially by the solder himself, and surely by your donations.

📊 Total amount collected in different currencies: 90474 UAH. Amount spent on the scope with mount is €2688 (which is about 100674 UAH). The deficiency was financed by me personally.

The scope was handled to the soldier and already serves him good for defending our freedom and our country!

Thank you all again! Together to the Victory!

Glory to Ukraine and its Heroes! 🇺🇦
