Report for additional nightvision device 📝

Jul 18, 2023

← Back to all posts

Many thanks to everyone who participated the fundraiser for the night vision device for soldiers of the 72nd brigade of Ukrainian armed forces.

A glorious soldier sent a photo with your gift, and conveys words of heartfelt thanks! Our falcons will hunt at night, and will see where the orcs are lurking, waiting for inevitable demise! 😉

📊 Some numbers:

A total of €1243 was collected in different currencies. And €1220 were spent on a device. The excess goes to the next fundraiser!

From myself and from the soldiers I sincerely thank everyone for your help!

Together to victory! 💪 Glory to Ukraine! 🇺🇦 Слава Україні! 🇺🇦
