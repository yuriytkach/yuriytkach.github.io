Fundraiser for Motorola Repeater 📻

Jan 20, 2023

← Back to all posts

Update: Fundraiser is closed! Thank you all!

Hello, friends! I am announcing another fundraiser - to improve the communication of our warriors! ⚡

I received a request from the 47th brigade: to find and buy a Motorola repeater 📻 As you understand, the need for a search was not accidentally emphasized separately - because it is not easy to find one on sale. It’s like with a gopher: everyone knows that it is somewhere, but try to see one…

A brief summary of the function of the repeater: this radio electronic device is installed at an intermediate point of the radio communication line to amplify the signals of the radios arriving at it and transmit them further. This allows you to significantly expand their coverage area by dozens of kilometers! 📡

In general, repeaters have many additional bonus functions... But the main thing is that at least one such device is able to provide high-quality communication regardless of the terrain and weather, over huge distances. 🗺️

This is vital! Especially now, when we have russian orcs 👹 trying to make breakthroughs at the same time in areas far from each other. They shall not pass! 🪦

I must admit that I have been looking for a Motorola repeater at an adequate price for quite a long time 🔎, especially considering that they are not often on sale at all. But finally - success! - the equipment is available, the guys approved the model, the price is the best - 3000 euros! ⚡

Now the matter is in our hands, and together with brigade 47 I am asking for your help in the form of donations acceptable to you. Every penny is the reliability of the communication of the Armed Forces and, accordingly, their power on the battlefield! This is the life and safety of defenders! This is our fastest Victory!!!

Thank you all in advance!
